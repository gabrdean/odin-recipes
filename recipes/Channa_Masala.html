Channa Masala

Description

Channa Masala is a popular indian entree. the appeal is found not only in its boastful flavor but its health benefits too. The dish is vegan but it may make you question whether it was cooked with meat or not. Indians do a great job with vegetarian foods as many religions from india focus on vegetarianism. Personally, they do it the best.

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 3/4 cups chickpeas
  • 1 1/2 cups Water

Saute and Blend

  • 1 tbs oil
  • 2-3 chopped onions
  • 1 tbs ginger
  • 1 tbs Garlic
  • 2 tomatoes chopped or tomato puree
  • 1/2 tbs red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp tumeric
  • 3/4 tsp Salt

Gravy

  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch of cinnamon
  • 3 cloves
  • 2 cardamoms
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi aka dried fenugreek leaves
  • 2 pinch amchur
  • 2 tbs coriander leaves
