Saheena

Description

Saheena is an indian food which found a new home in its voyage to trinidad and tobago. The Vegan delight warms you with a oil slicked crispy golden crunch followed by a secretion of earthly green juices spiralled with punchy spices of the native land.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup ground Split Peas
  • 1 lb chopped Spinach
  • 1 tbs Instant Yeast
  • 1 tsp Himalayan Salt
  • 2 tbs minced Garlic
  • 2 tbs minced Bandhania
  • 1 tsp Roasted Ground Cumin Geera
  • 1 Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Tumeric
  • 1 cup lukewarm Water
  • Vegetable oil for Frying
