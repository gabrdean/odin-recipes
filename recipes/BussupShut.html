Bussup Shut

Description

Bussup Shut is a popular breading found in the trini streets. Often served with some chana, this gold soft doughy sweet delight will add the perfect balance for any of the flavorful sauce or filling. Very similar to Roti. Now don't read this wrong, eating this alone is more than enough satifaction to salivate your glands urging for more.

Ingredients

Kneading the Dough

  • 4 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups lukewarm Water
  • 1 tbs Vegetable oil

Filling the Dough

  • 4 tbs clarified butter AKA Ghee
  • Fllour for dusting

To Cook

  • 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil or Butter
